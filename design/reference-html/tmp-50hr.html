50 Hour Yoga Teacher Training in Bali

01.

About 50 Hour Hatha YTT

The 50-hour Hatha Yoga Teacher Training at Bali YTTC is a foundational course perfect for those new to yoga or seeking to deepen their understanding of traditional Hatha practice. Located in peaceful Ubud, surrounded by nature, this course provides the essential building blocks for your yoga journey.

02.

What Makes This Course Special

  • Focus on traditional Hatha Yoga fundamentals
  • Small class sizes (maximum 10 students)
  • Beginner-friendly approach
  • Experienced certified instructors
  • Beautiful natural setting in Ubud
  • Authentic Balinese cultural experiences
syllabus

50 HOUR YTT CURRICULUM

Vinyasa flow ​ & Hatha Yoga

These Classes Include Asana Practice, teaching each Sun Salutation in-depth, learning and practicing Led and Mysore style Ashtanga Primary series, how to create your own Vinyasa flow sequence, How these styles can impact one’s life physically as well as mentally.

  • Warm-up – Pawan mukta asana series
  • Traditional Hatha Sun-salutation and Ashtanga Sun-salutation
  • Variations in a Vinyasa Flow
  • The concept of Asanas, varieties, and effects
  • How to Create Vinyasa Yoga
  • Standing sequence and Standing postures
  • Seated Postures,
  • supine Postures
  • prone poses,
  • Inversions, the closing sequence
  • Vinyasa sequences based on themes.
Alignment and Adjustment

These classes are dedicated to refining the understanding of asana.

  • How to safely go in and out of a posture
  • Anatomy of a yoga posture
  • Benefits of a yoga posture
  • Alignment & modification
  • How to use props in various postures.
  • How to teach ashtanga yoga and vinyasa flow classes.
  • You will start practice teaching from day one,
  • starting from one-to-one to larger groups.
  • You will be supervised and guided by senior teachers.
  • Hands-on adjustment workshops:

In these classes, we will teach you the art of adjustment.

Mantra, Meditation & Pranayama

Our Meditation and pranayama classes are built on experience, and they can definitely help people to heal themselves mentally & physically. In these classes, you will learn all these important Pranayama techniques and different meditation techniques along with Mantra chantings.

 
Anatomy

These lessons will upgrade your understanding of this physical body, How these Asana, Pranayama, and Meditation classes help us and can be used as Yoga Therapy for Wellbeing. In these classes, you will learn Skeleton, Muscular, Respiratory, Digestive Systems and Physiology.

Acro Yoga Workshop

Experience the magic of Acro Yoga with us at the end of your first week! This workshop is perfect for complete beginners—no experience needed. We’ll start with the basics: learning to balance, build trust with partners, and have plenty of fun together. Our certified instructors will guide you step-by-step, making sure everyone feels safe and supported. Whether you’re new to yoga or looking to try something playful and different, you’ll love discovering what your body can do!
